Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a soccer player who has has scored 725 senior career goals for club and country. Moreover, he is also the first player in history to win four European Golden Shoes. Collectively, Ronaldo has won between 28 and 31 trophies in his career, including six league titles, five European Cups and one European Championship.
